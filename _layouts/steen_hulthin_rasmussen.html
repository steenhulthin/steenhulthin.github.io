Steen Hulthin Rasmussen

I'm a software developer and/or GIS dude. My main expertise is working with digital map, spatial data and GIS products at all levels of the technology stack.

In my work life I'm the CEO/code monkey/Key Account Manager of Percipio. I have standard CV available for download with a standard resume of some of the things I have done professionally even though I'm not a big fan of standard CVs.

Copenhagen, Denmark is my home town. I live there together with my girlfriend and between 1 and 3 children. I really enjoy running, biking and chocolate. I love life.

I'm also a failed chess and guitar player, a Dane (and actually half-swedish, thanks to Mom), around 40 (thanks to Mom and Dad), bald (thanks to Dad) and a chocolate lover (did I mention that already?).

Projects

I maintain a few Open Source Software projects:

In the serious catagory are:

  • MapBasicUnit - A unit testing library for the MapBasic language. MapBasic is a language for the desktop GIS MapInfo.
  • reStructuredText_NPP - Syntax highlighting for reStructuredText for Notepad++.
  • FreeMarker_NPP - Syntax highlighting for the template language FreeMarker for Notepad++.

In the mostly for fun category are:

  • The Anti Region Legion - a coding style movement for a better C# world.
  • LOLCODE_NPP - Syntax highlighting for LOLCODE for Notepad++.
  • CollatzConjecture.Net - a .net library for calculating solutions to Collatz Conjecture problems.

Besides writing code I also write blog posts. The topic is mostly technical stuff related to software development and/or GIS, but occasionally also about management and team work. You can find my blog on http://steen.hulthin.dk/blog/.
